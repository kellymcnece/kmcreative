ABOUT ME

I am a freelance Web Developer based out of Arkanas. I began my love for code at a very young age through a popular website, at the time, MySpace. I fell in love with how amazing some of the users profiles looked and I needed to know how they did it. And just like that, I was hooked.

Fast-forward some years later, and here I am. The love is still there, and the desire to create a beautiful page is even more intense. So here I am now, doing front-end development and am able to use my love for details to create chic & clean code.
